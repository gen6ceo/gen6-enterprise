[ THE FOUNDER ]

Built from the ground up.

"My name is Trey Hill Jr. I started GEN6 Enterprise in Fort Worth, Texas with a single decision: build something real.

I didn't come from money. I came from experience — hard lessons, wrong turns, and the kind of clarity that only comes after you've had to rebuild yourself from scratch.

GEN6 exists because I saw a gap nobody was filling. Traveling professionals were solving housing and transportation separately, every single assignment. Paying a premium for the inconvenience. Nobody was packaging both under one roof and building a real company around it.

I built the system. I tested it. I'm running it right now in Fort Worth and Dallas. And I'm building it to scale."

— Trey Hill Jr, Founder & CEO
