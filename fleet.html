[ FAQ ]

Questions, answered.

What types of vehicles are available?

Our fleet includes reliable sedans and SUVs suited for daily commuting, plus trucks for workforce and construction assignments. Every vehicle is vetted, maintained, and cleaned before pickup. Tell us your assignment needs when you book and we'll match you with the right vehicle.

How does weekly billing work?

Weekly rentals bill automatically to the card on file at the start of each rental week. You'll get a receipt by email every cycle. If your assignment has a set end date, we'll prorate or align billing to it — no surprise charges.

Do you require insurance?

Yes — insurance is the one requirement we don't flex on, because it protects the vehicle you're driving. Use your own auto policy if it covers rentals, or add GEN6 coverage to your booking for an added cost. Either way, there's no deposit.

Can my company pay directly?

Yes. We support direct corporate billing for staffing agencies, construction companies, and corporate travel programs. Include your company details when you book and we'll set up invoicing with your finance team.

Is there a deposit?

No. We don't take deposits on vehicles or housing. Your documents and insurance are what secure the booking — meet the requirements and the keys are yours.

What happens if I need to extend my rental?

Just reach out before your current term ends — a call or text is enough. Extensions are our most common request and we prioritize existing renters over new bookings, so your vehicle stays yours.

Are vehicles available in cities outside Fort Worth?

We currently operate in Fort Worth and Dallas, with Garland service expanding. If your assignment is elsewhere in Texas, book anyway — we're growing into new markets based on demand and can sometimes coordinate delivery.
