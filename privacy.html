Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

GEN6 Enterprise LLC ("GEN6", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through gen6enterprise.com and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on this site, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, assignment details, and property information. We also collect standard technical data like browser type and pages visited.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your housing, vehicle rental, property, or partnership inquiry
  • To match you with available housing or vehicles
  • To send occasional updates about GEN6 services (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our website and services

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We use trusted service providers (such as our CRM and email platform) to process inquiries and communications on our behalf. Those providers only receive the information needed to perform their function.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry information for as long as needed to serve you and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at hello@gen6enterprise.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@gen6enterprise.com or on Instagram at @gen6enterprise. GEN6 Enterprise LLC, Fort Worth, Texas.
