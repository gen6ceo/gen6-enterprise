[ STRAIGHT ANSWERS ]

The questions that actually matter.

You're trusting someone in a new city with your housing and your wheels. You should get straight answers before you send anyone a dollar.

What do I need to qualify?

A valid driver's license, proof of your assignment or income (a travel contract, employer letter, or recent pay stubs), and verifiable insurance for vehicle rentals. Documents check out, you're booked — no waiting list, no lengthy application.

Are the photos the actual unit and vehicle I'll get?

Yes — always. What you see in your booking confirmation is the exact unit and the exact vehicle with your name on it. No bait-and-switch, no "similar to" fine print.

What happens if my assignment gets cancelled or cut short?

Assignments change — that's the industry. Tell us as soon as you know, show the change from your agency or employer, and we'll work the terms with you instead of against you. The exact notice terms are written plainly in your agreement before you sign.

How fast can I actually be set up?

If your documents are ready and a unit and vehicle are available for your dates, setup happens in as little as 24 hours — and we aim to have keys and an address in your hands before your start date. Tell us your dates first; we'll tell you honestly what's available.

Who do I contact when something breaks at 9pm?

The owner. Directly. Every GEN6 client has a direct line to Trey — the same person who set up your unit and handed you the keys. No ticket systems, no "we'll pass that along."
